Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

At New Leaf, accessible from https://new-leaf.net, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information from users. We may collect basic, non-personal analytics data and advertising information through third-party services like Google AdSense.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to enhance your experience and serve relevant ads through third-party providers. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their respective advertisements and links that appear on our site. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their campaigns and personalize advertising content.

To learn more about these third-party policies, please review their individual privacy policies directly.
